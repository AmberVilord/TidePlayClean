Tide GoClean Logo Concept

Theme: Global Cleaning for the Future.

Primary Logo

Circular globe symbol with subtle global grid, orange Tide wave, and forward arrow for progress. Built to scale across app icons, stations, product packaging, and keychain device branding.

Structure

  • Primary shape: circular globe to represent the planet and connected network
  • Element 1: 3 horizontal and 3 vertical curved grid lines for global infrastructure
  • Element 2: slightly upward curved Tide-orange wave across center
  • Element 3: forward arrow inside wave to signal innovation and motion

Color Palette

  • Tide Orange: #F25C05
  • Deep Ocean Blue: #003A8F
  • Fresh Sky Blue: #4CC3F1
  • White accents: arrow and contrast details

Symbol-Only Version

Use this mark where space is tight or icon-first branding is preferred.

App Icon Version

Deep blue background with the globe symbol, orange wave, and white arrow for high small-size readability.

Motion Concept

  • 1. Globe lines appear
  • 2. Wave sweeps across
  • 3. Arrow advances forward

This sequence reinforces cleaning in motion and future-forward technology.
