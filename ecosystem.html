GoClean in Action Overview

The system combines physical tools, public stations, and app intelligence into one connected loop.

1) GoClean Clip

Portable keychain cleaner and smart access key. Approximate size: 4.25 in × 2 in × 1.4 in with rounded, durable edges.

  • Mini Tide stain remover pen
  • 15 Biodegradable wipe
  • Fabric refresh spray
  • Secure NFC for station tap, app sync, and optional payment

2) GoClean Stations

Community cleaning hubs for parks, universities, transit hubs, sports complexes, event venues, and shopping centers.

  • Stain spray tool
  • Low-pressure fabric rinse sprayer
  • Quick dry fan
  • Smart tap panel for Clip or phone app activation

3) GoClean App

Digital hub connecting stain intelligence, navigation, impact metrics, and account controls.

  • AI stain scanner: grass, coffee, grease, paint, food
  • Station map with nearby distance view
  • Dashboard: stains cleaned, clothes saved, water saved
  • Payment and refill subscription management

Smart Interactions and Payment Models

  1. Tap Clip at station to activate spray, rinse, and dry cycle.
  2. Use clip as contactless key with linked card, wallet, or app account.
  3. Tap Clip to phone to open app guidance and refill reminders.

Models can include free community stations, pay-per-use stations, or membership plans. Example: tap to start a $0.50 cycle.

Privacy by Design

  • Clip does not store personal identity, location history, or financial details.
  • Anonymous mode supports tap-and-clean without account creation.
  • Sensitive data remains encrypted in app infrastructure.
  • Stations track only anonymous metrics: cleaning count, water usage, refill levels.
  • Users can view, export, and delete their data.
