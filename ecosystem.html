GoClean in Action

The system combines physical tools, public stations, and app intelligence into one connected loop.

01

GoClean Clip

Portable keychain cleaner and smart access key. Approximate size: 4.25 in × 2 in × 1.4 in with rounded, durable edges.

  • Mini Tide stain remover pen
  • 15 Biodegradable wipe
  • Fabric refresh spray
  • Secure NFC for station tap, app sync, and optional payment
02

GoClean Stations

Community cleaning hubs for parks, universities, transit hubs, sports complexes, event venues, and shopping centers.

Cleaning Hardware

  • Detergent mist spray tool for targeted stain treatment
  • Low-pressure fabric rinse sprayer to gently remove detergent and loosened stains
  • Angled cleaning pad surface where users can press fabric to safely scrub or blot stains
  • Replaceable cleaning pads stored within the station for hygiene and easy maintenance
  • Quick dry fan to help remove moisture after cleaning

Access and Payments

  • Smart tap panel that activates the station when scanned with the GoClean Clip or mobile app
  • Optional contactless card reader for users who do not have a GoClean Clip or prefer direct payment
03

GoClean App

Digital hub connecting stain intelligence, navigation, impact metrics, and account controls.

  • AI stain scanner: grass, coffee, grease, paint, food, and more!
  • Station map with nearby distance view
  • Dashboard: stains cleaned, clothes saved, water saved
  • Payment and refill subscription management
04

Smart Interactions and Payment Models

  • Tap the GoClean Clip at the station to activate the cleaning cycle, which enables the detergent mist, rinse sprayer, wipe dispenser, and quick dry fan.
  • Use the Clip as a contactless key, linked to a debit/credit card, mobile wallet, or GoClean app account for automatic station access.
  • Tap the Clip to a smartphone to open the GoClean app, which provides stain identification guidance, cleaning instructions, and refill reminders for wipes or detergent cartridges.
  • Optional contactless card reader allows users without a GoClean Clip to activate the station using a debit card, credit card, or mobile wallet.
  • Flexible payment models allow stations to operate in different environments:
  • Pay-per-use stations for quick cleaning cycles
  • Membership or subscription plans for frequent users
05

Privacy by Design

  • Clip does not store personal identity, location history, or financial details.
  • Anonymous mode supports tap-and-clean without account creation.
  • Sensitive data remains encrypted in app infrastructure.
  • Stations track only anonymous metrics: cleaning count, water usage, refill levels.
  • Users can view, export, and delete their data.
