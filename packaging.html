Biodegradable Packaging Strategy

Packaging is designed to cut plastic use while preserving premium usability and durability.

Clip Starter Kit Packaging

  • Molded recycled paper-pulp shell and tray
  • No plastic window films
  • Plant-based adhesive seals
  • Soy-based inks for all print materials

Refill Packaging

  • Compostable paper sleeves
  • Plant-fiber internal lining
  • Compact format to reduce shipping footprint

Station Refill Logistics

  • Returnable aluminum refill canisters
  • Service-loop pickup and refill model
  • Lower lifecycle plastic consumption

Sustainability Loop Impact

  • Reduces unnecessary full-wash cycles
  • Extends clothing lifespan
  • Cuts single-use plastic packaging through refill systems
  • Supports measurable environmental dashboards in-app
