Example User Journey

Scenario: A college student spills coffee on a hoodie before class.

  1. The student pulls out the GoClean Clip and sprays a small amount of detergent mist directly onto the stain.
  2. A wipe from the clip is then used to gently lift the stain from the fabric.
  3. At the nearest GoClean Station on campus, the Clip is tapped to activate a quick cleaning cycle.
  4. The station applies a very small spray of detergent during a targeted rinse, provides a disposeable cleaning pad for scrubbing, and finishes with a short dry cycle so the hoodie is ready to wear before class.
  5. The GoClean App automatically logs the cleaning event and updates the user's sustainability dashboard, tracking water saved and full laundry cycles avoided.

Fast Action

Immediate spot treatment helps prevent set-in stains and unnecessary full washes.

Connected Access

Clip, station, and app create one seamless flow from problem to resolution.

Measurable Impact

Each cleaning event contributes to trackable clothes-saved and water-saved outcomes.
