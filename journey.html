Example User Journey

Scenario: a college student spills coffee on their hoodie before class.

  1. They use the GoClean Clip to treat the stain immediately.
  2. They tap the Clip at a GoClean Station on campus.
  3. The station sprays, rinses, and dries the fabric in one short cycle.
  4. The GoClean App logs the clean and updates sustainability metrics.

Fast Action

Immediate spot treatment helps prevent set-in stains and unnecessary full washes.

Connected Access

Clip, station, and app create one seamless flow from problem to resolution.

Measurable Impact

Each cleaning event contributes to trackable clothes-saved and water-saved outcomes.
